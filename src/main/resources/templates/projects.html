SoundConnect

Working collaboratively with a group of developers utilizing the spotify API to design, plan, build, and deploy a mobile-responsive full-stack social music application. Through morning stand-ups and afternoon demos, we leveraged an Agile workflow to execute the full development lifecycle through deployment to AWS.
